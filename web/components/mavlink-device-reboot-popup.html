Warning: Device Reboot

Rebooting the flight controller will temporarily interrupt all operations and connections.

What happens during reboot:

  • Flight controller will shut down all active connections
  • System will perform a clean restart (approximately 30-60 seconds)
  • All configurations and settings will be preserved
  • MAVLink connections will be re-established automatically

Before proceeding, ensure:

  • Vehicle is in a safe state (disarmed if applicable)
  • No critical operations are in progress
  • Power supply is stable and secure
